 VSC8562_PHY_STATUS  
0x10
 Status bits related to VSC8562 (valid on FPGA1 only)
 7..0 PG_TIMED_OUT READY RESET REFCLK_EN COMA_MODE PG_V2P5 PG_V1P0 EN_V1P0
Name Bit(s) Access Reset Description
PG_TIMED_OUT 7 r 0x0 Either the V1P0 or V2P5 rail has failed to come up. V1P0 is first in the sequence, its PG is the EN for V2P5.
READY 6 r 0x0 The VSC8562 has been brought out of reset and is ready for software control
RESET 5 r 0x0 Value inverted to drive FPGA1_TO_PHY_RESET_L.
REFCLK_EN 4 r 0x0 Value of FPGA1_TO_PHY_REFCLK_EN
COMA_MODE 3 r 0x0 Value of FPGA1_TO_PHY_COMA_MODE
PG_V2P5 2 r 0x0 Power Good of V2P5_PHY_A2
PG_V1P0 1 r 0x0 Power Good of V1P0_PHY_A2
EN_V1P0 0 r 0x0 Enable for 1.0V PHY VR
 VSC8562_PHY_CTRL  
0x11
 Control bits related to VSC8562 (valid on FPGA1 only)
 7..0 RSVD CLEAR_POWER_FAULT COMA_MODE EN
Name Bit(s) Access Reset Description
